About the Exam
The CompTIA DataSys+ certification exam will certify the successful candidate has the knowledge and skills required
to deploy, manage, and maintain databases, including employing the fundamentals of scripting and programming in a
database environment while using security and business continuity best practices.
ANSI ACCREDITATION
The CompTIA DataSys+ exam is accredited by ANSI to show compliance with the ISO 17024 standard and, as such,
undergoes regular reviews and updates to the exam objectives.
EXAM DEVELOPMENT
CompTIA exams result from subject matter expert workshops and industry-wide survey results regarding the skills and
knowledge required of an IT professional.
